Welcome to the game of Rock, Paper, Scissors! The objective of this game is to be the first player to win 5 rounds. Here's how to play:

On each turn, you will select either rock, paper, or scissors by clicking on the corresponding icon displayed on the screen.

Rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats rock.

If both you and the computer choose the same gesture, the round is considered a tie and no one wins the point.

After each round, the winner will be announced and a point will be awarded to that player. The first player to reach 5 points wins the game.

Good luck and have fun! May the best player win.
